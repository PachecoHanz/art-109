About Me

Hello! I'm Hanz Pacheco, a Filipino 3D artist based in San Francisco. I create environments filled with rich textures, lighting, and detail. My goal is to blend technical skill with artistry to build immersive worlds.

I specialize in Blender, Substance Painter, and After Effects for modeling and 3D scenes. For digital illustrations, I use Photoshop, Premiere, and Clip Studio.
